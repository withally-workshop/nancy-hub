Supabase
Checking...
1.Go to supabase.com/dashboard and check your project is active (not paused).
2.If paused, click Restore project. Free tier pauses after 1 week of inactivity.
3.If still broken, check that SUPABASE_URL and SUPABASE_KEY in shared/hub.js match your project settings.
GAS Script
Checking...
1.Open your Google Apps Script project.
2.Click Deploy → Manage deployments and confirm the script is deployed as a Web App.
3.Make sure Execute as: Me and Who has access: Anyone is set.
4.Copy the deployment URL and update SCRIPT_URL in shared/hub.js.
Gmail
Checking...
1.Open your Google Apps Script project.
2.In the editor, find or add this function and click Run:
function testGmail() { GmailApp.search('in:inbox',0,1); }
3.A popup will appear — click Review Permissions → Allow.
4.Come back here and click Recheck.
Slack
Checking...
1.Click your avatar/name in the top-right to open your profile menu.
2.Find the My Slack section and paste your Slack user token (starts with xoxp-).
3.To get a token: go to api.slack.com/apps, create an app, add channels:history + channels:read + users:read scopes, then install to your workspace.
4.Click Save in My Slack, then click Recheck below.
