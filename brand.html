About Hello Nancy
Hello Nancy is CareNBloom's sexual wellness brand — here to make pleasure approachable, fun, and completely shame-free. We believe that exploring your own pleasure is a form of self-care, and that everyone deserves access to quality products and honest education to do it on their own terms.

Our products are thoughtfully designed, beginner-friendly, and packaged with privacy in mind. Our brand is built on the belief that wellness includes the full spectrum of joy — and that conversations about pleasure should feel as natural as any other part of health. From TikTok-viral toys to carefully curated bundles, Hello Nancy meets people where they are: curious, open, and ready to explore.

Platform Guidance
TikTok
Hook in the first 3 seconds — no slow intros
Use trending audio where it fits naturally
Blend educational + entertaining ("edutainment")
No explicit content — follow Community Guidelines strictly
Captions should reinforce the hook, not repeat it
Comments are part of the content strategy — reply and engage
Instagram
Maintain an aesthetic, cohesive grid — colors and mood matter
Product highlights and lifestyle content in equal measure
Stories for polls, questions, and behind-the-scenes
Reels for reach — treat like short TikToks with grid appeal
Captions can be longer and more storytelling-forward than TikTok
Hashtag strategy: mix brand, category, and trending tags

Do's & Don'ts
Do
Use inclusive, body-neutral language that welcomes all identities
Lead with the benefit or feeling, not just the feature
Keep social content educational AND entertaining
Emphasise privacy, discretion, and safety at every touchpoint
Be playful, punny, and cheeky — the brand has a sense of humor
Follow platform community guidelines — always
Celebrate the full range of bodies, relationships, and identities
Use real product names (Avo, Berri, Namii 2) consistently
Don't
Use explicit sexual language or graphic descriptions in public-facing content
Use shame, guilt, or embarrassment as a hook — even jokingly
Write in a clinical, medical, or overly formal tone
Default to gendered language that excludes non-binary or trans audiences
Make product claims that can't be substantiated (e.g. medical benefits)
Copy competitor styles or voice — Hello Nancy has its own distinct personality
Post on platforms without checking content guidelines first
Be preachy or lecture-y — we educate and empower, we don't lecture
