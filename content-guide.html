How to use this guide
📸
General Shooting Shots
Tap a card to open the shot checklist for that subject. Check off each scene as you capture it on set.
🎬
Content Shooting Guides
Tap a card to browse video references for that content type. Each video has a shot breakdown and a ready-to-use script.
👆
Inside a guide
Tap Shots to see the timestamp breakdown. Tap Script for the Nancified version. Tap See Full Guide for the complete context.
